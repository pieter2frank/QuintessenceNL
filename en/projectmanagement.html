Quintessence Project Management

Are you planning a new project and do you require expert advice? Quintessence Project Management may be just what you need. We specialise in all aspects of the project management process, including project applications, proposals, evaluation, guidance, implementation and financial auditing. In this process we adhere strictly to the international DFID (Department for International Development) standards. We are also able to step in and provide assistance with projects that are already in a running phase.

Projects in developing countries
Our involvement can extend to a wide range of projects and contexts, including areas such as micro finance, large-scale building projects, education and environmental management and conservation both in terms of short-term, direct aid or longer-term reconstruction or rehabilitation processes. In the case of implementing projects in developing countries in particular, regular financial and project auditing is of the utmost importance. Foundations, large private donors and corporate businesses may demand accurate and regular progress reports to ensure the effectiveness of a project. Have the funds that were made available been utilised towards achieving the project’s goals? Is the project able to meet its objectives? And if not, why is this the case? What are the (long-term) outcomes of the project? How can the project further improve its impact and effectiveness? These questions and more are handled by Quintessence Project Management.

Other projects
A project may also mean the implementation of an activity that meets the objectives of a particular organization.
Some other examples where you may need our specialised team include:

  • Fundraising campaigns to raise support and awareness for your cause
  • Activities aimed at fostering links with the public such as, benefit concerts or gala evenings
  • Open days for sponsors and volunteers
  • Other fundraising activities, such as sponsored sports events, etc.

Quintessence has exactly the right expertise, valuable contacts, the right network as well as a team of specialists who are able to organise the right event for your needs and wishes, from start to finish. While you remain focussed on the important tasks within your organization, you can contact us by completing a contact sheet and we will get in touch with you as soon as possible.
