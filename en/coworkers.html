Co-workers and affiliated services

Henk Koppelaar – Managing Director, Management and Consultancy

After a successful career in business (Management and ICT) I chose a new career path in 2000 in the non profit sector. Over the past ten years, I have gained a wealth of experience in a wide range of areas related to the management of charity organizations. With my in depth knowledge both in business and non profit sectors I am well equipped to assist organizations to further their own developments. Whether this assistance relates to management issues, international (aid) projects, internal organization or fundraising, you can rest assured that both myself and my team of specialists will do the utmost to make your organization more successful. In addition, I have gained managerial experience in various fields (including musical and cultural industries, health care and education) and have numerous years of international experience in Western and Eastern Europe, America and Asia. I am also a regular speaker at conferences at home in the Netherlands and abroad, and I have authored several publications.

Henk Koppelaar – Managing Director, Management and Consultancy

For 17 years I worked as consultant for development cooperation in the Philippines. Since 2003 I have been involved in several aid organizations in Switzerland. I either served as CEO or as Head of the international programs department. My professional experiences together with my academic studies and further education qualify me as NPO-Manager, development expert and consultant for organizational development. Over the years I have accumulated rich experiences as consultant either addressing organizational issues or matters concerning projects and programs. My consultancies brought me to the following countries: Philippines, Indonesia, Bangladesh, Afghanistan, Uganda, South Sudan, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Democratic Republic of Congo, Cameroon and Malawi.

Mr. DirkJan Warnaar – Audio, video and photo production
Warnaar Studio creates evocative documentaries and film presentations that clearly portray the strengths of a business, charity organization or school with passion and charisma. The basis for these films is rooted in extensive conversations about the goals and motivation of your organization, using a collaborative approach. In other words, your ideas fuel the creativity and content of the documentary or film. The uniqueness and strength of your organization or charity provide the inspiration for an equally unique video production. Warnaar Studio has developed its own style and concept. This is an ideal opportunity to acquire a beautiful, rich in content, audiovisual or photographic presentation of your organization or charity.
