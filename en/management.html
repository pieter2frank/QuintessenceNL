Quintessence Management and Consultancy

The focus of Quintessence Management and Consultancy is providing advice and guidance, aimed to maximise the effectiveness of charities and non profit organizations. We achieve this through implementing customised Management Development programmes, Interim Management, and Vision, Mission and Strategy Development programmes. In specific cases, we are also able to provide Mediation and Conflict-resolution.

A practical outcome of good managerial decision-making is the implementation of decisions aimed to improve an organization’s efficiency and goal-orientation.

Quintessence Organizational Development
To this end, Quintessence Organizational Development provides objective advice and guidance aimed to enhance the primary processes within a charity organization. Depending on the assignment, Quintessence analyses and evaluates one or more aspects within the running of an organization and provides concrete suggestions to improve or adapt the organization’s structure or to implement other suitable changes. These changes could relate to areas such as ICT or the use and implementation of specific software as well as financial administration, communication and PR. For each of these areas, Quintessence is able to provide objective, expert guidance. This objective standpoint counters so-called ‘tunnel vision’ and ensures all relevant points of view are considered in the decision-making process.
