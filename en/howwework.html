Company formula

Many umbrella organizations within the charity sector offer services and support to their members but not to other parties. Also, within the charity sector there exist a number of consultancy agencies. Often these agencies apply exorbitant rates and are therefore only available to wealthy trust funds and large charity organizations. Quintessence aims to fill this void by offering specialist knowledge and expertise applying rates that are modest, even for the charity sector.

Affordable expertise for the whole spectrum of charity organizations

Quintessence targets the whole spectrum of charity organizations, large or small-scale, locally as well as internationally. ANBI recognises more than 6200 charity organizations. In addition, many of these charity organizations have an international component: a charity could be part of a larger organization based abroad and/or has projects in developing nations in different parts of the world. With its team of specialists who have a broad international experience, Quintessence is able to guide projects in any place in the world and in many contexts, including disaster situations, or in setting up and coordinating localised branches of an organization. There are no limits to Quintessence’s involvement in the charity sector.

For further information, please contact us through the contact sheet on this website. We can also be reached by telephone and we are more than happy to meet with you personally to explain to you more fully the benefits of our organization.

Managing relationships

Good preparation is essential to achieve results. When taking on a new assignment, the first thing we do is ask a lot questions. Why? Because we take time to familiarise ourselves with the inner workings of your organization and its team, you can rest assured that the project or assignment will run smoothly. In addition, we honour our agreements regarding quality, rates and deadlines, so that you will not be faced with unpleasant surprises or delays.

A successful project requires meticulous work

We feel equally strongly that once you have entrusted an activity to us, you should not experience any added concerns that detract from your organization’s main activities. Initiating and implementing campaigns is no simple task: a successful project requires meticulous work! Several factors that are crucial for the success of a project require constant monitoring and expertise. This is where Quintessence helps. Depending on your particular needs and wishes we guide the entire process of a project or particular project phases.

Saving time with a proactive approach

Because we are working with a team of specialists from a wide range of disciplines, we are able to support organizations effectively and efficiently in a multitude of areas within the organizational process. As well as efficiency this allows you to save time in developing and running campaigns. We manage external staff and deadlines, we ensure quality control and prevent unnecessary expenditure and delays. In order to maximise productivity and income we search for ways to improve your campaigns. Although we do not deal directly with design, graphic production or text writing, we have an extensive network that is highly experienced in these skills. Wherever possible, we apply a proactive approach to advise and find you the necessary contacts and connections.
