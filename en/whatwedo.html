What we do...

We assist non profit/fundraising organizations in organizational issues and management related areas. We offer advice relating to management, policy, fundraising and (international) projects. The charity sector experiences constant pressure from public opinion in terms of its transparency and (financial) accountability. Our current changing economic climate of decreasing income and increasing expenses forces organizations to scrutinise their expenditure more closely and to search for alternatives. To meet these needs, many organizations require independent, specialist advice. We aim to meet this need in terms of:

Management

We...
  • observe and analyse the effectiveness of all aspects of management and offer advice for the improvement of management operations and strategies
  • advise and coach organizations towards realising and achieving their objectives
  • provide assistance where problem-solving and conflict resolution is required with management and other staff.
  • support the Board of Directors in establishing vision, mission and strategy of the charity
  • are a professional soundboard in terms of annual planning and budgeting

Fundraising

We ...

  • offer feedback on response percentages and other collected data through the careful analysis of data from previous campaigns and objective market research.
  • provide assistance and advice in the launching of new campaigns. Who should you approach, how do you reach this target group, and what do you emphasise in order to achieve the best results?

Project Management

We ...

  • have the knowledge and experience to develop as well as implement project proposals.
  • offer (temporary) specialised knowledge and support as well as manpower to assist you in the field.
  • deliver suitable progress reports, financial audits during an assignment and a detailed evaluation upon its completion.
  • assist you with the organization of large public events and campaigns.


Get support without adding unnecessary expenses

Sounds impossible? Not if you rely on us. We have a wealth of experience in organising PR activities such as productions, concerts and other events. The non profit sector is characterised by the fast flow of creativity and new ideas. Numerous marketing strategies are regularly tested and used to inspire the public and corporate businesses to contribute to various charities and causes. However, our experience tells us that these strategies and initiatives are not always used to their full effect and that they do not always run smoothly, due to, for instance, communication breakdowns or an insufficient understanding of the particular needs of an organization. Quintessence understands these challenges and guarantees a service without unwanted surprises.

Our core values

Surprises are not part of Quintessence’s business policy. As an organization, we value openness and clear communication in all our activities. Quality and reliability form part of our core. These values ensure a client-centred approach. We become your partner working towards achieving a common goal.
